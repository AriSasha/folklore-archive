What is Abantu Tales?

Due to changes in times, exposure and different societal influences, our cultures are slowly being eroded and replaced by other adopted cultures. The generations to come are going to need an authentic source of information regarding their cultures and virtues that governed,grounded and nurtured those before them.

That is what Abantu Tales is about, a virtual space to impart and preserve our cultures through stories, poems, songs and any other kind of information that will enable those after us to stay in touch with their roots.
